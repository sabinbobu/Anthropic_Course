STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

1. ENGAGEMENT IMPROVEMENT (Priority: CRITICAL)

2. CUSTOMER SERVICE OPTIMIZATION (Priority: HIGH)
   • Problem: Higher CS interactions correlate with 28% higher churn
   • Actions:
     - Analyze root causes of customer support tickets
     - Implement proactive outreach after 2+ support interactions
     - Improve self-service resources to reduce friction
     - Track resolution satisfaction scores

   • Problem: Basic tier has 43.5% churn vs 24.1% for Premium
   • Actions:

   • Actions:
     - Expand Documentary and Comedy content libraries (lowest churn)
     - Cross-promote genres to diversify viewing habits
     - Survey Horror/Thriller fans to understand content gaps

5. EARLY WARNING SYSTEM (Priority: CRITICAL)
   • Implement predictive churn model monitoring:
     - Weekly scoring of all active customers
